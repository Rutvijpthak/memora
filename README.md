## 📸 What is Memora?

After every wedding, thread ceremony, birthday or family celebration — photographers deliver **thousands of photos**. Going through 5,000 to 10,000 photos manually to pick the best 200 takes days, is exhausting, and you still miss moments.

**Memora solves this completely.**

It analyses every single photo using AI — scoring sharpness, lighting, facial expressions, happiness, duplicate burst shots, and timeline coverage — then automatically selects your best photos and exports them album-ready. What used to take days now takes minutes.

> 💡 *Built after a real problem — a family ceremony with 7,000 photos and no time to go through them all.*

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 AI-Powered Photo Scoring
Every photo is scored across multiple dimensions simultaneously:
- **Sharpness** — centre-weighted analysis, portrait mode safe
- **Exposure** — removes too dark or too bright photos automatically
- **Face detection** — prefers photos with clear faces
- **Emotion scoring** — detects happiness and genuine smiles using DeepFace AI
- **Resolution** — prefers higher quality, higher megapixel images

### 🔁 Smart Duplicate Removal
- Automatically detects and removes burst shots using perceptual hashing
- Keeps only the sharpest, best-exposed photo from each burst
- Adjustable sensitivity — strict to relaxed

### 🕐 Timeline Diversity
- Ensures your selection covers the entire event — arrival to farewell
- No more albums that are 80% one moment and missing everything else
- Intelligent time-bucket selection algorithm

### 🔒 100% Private & Offline
- Everything runs entirely on your computer
- Zero photos uploaded to any server — ever
- No internet required for analysis
- Your originals are never moved, renamed or deleted
- Complete peace of mind with sensitive family photos

### 📁 Supports 20+ Image Formats
| Standard | RAW Formats |
|----------|-------------|
| JPG, JPEG | CR2, CR3 (Canon) |
| PNG | NEF (Nikon) |
| HEIC, HEIF | ARW (Sony) |
| WEBP | DNG (Universal RAW) |
| BMP | ORF (Olympus) |
| TIFF, TIF | RW2 (Panasonic) |
| GIF | RAF (Fujifilm) |
